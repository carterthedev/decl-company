Who We Are

Founded in August 2017, our company has rapidly gained a reputation as a trusted brand within the industry. Our main warehouse, strategically located in Ndeiya Mboroti town in Kiambu County, serves as the central hub for our operations with an additional outlet shop in Kikuyu town.

At Diamond Engineering, we understand the unique needs and challenges faced by small hardware businesses when it comes to sourcing interior decoration products. To cater to these requirements, we have established strong partnerships with reputable local and international manufacturers. This allows us to offer a wide range of high-quality products to our clients, ensuring that they can stock their inventory with confidence.

With trusted global partnerships and our own manufacturing line, Diamond Engineering delivers a complete, quality-driven interior decoration solution for small hardware businesses

Our product catalog includes a diverse range of interior decoration items, such as MDF boards, ceiling boards, plywood, block boards, frames, gypsum boards, and accessories. In addition to distribution, we have also ventured into manufacturing our own products, including wooden doors, gypsum cornices, channels, studs and medallions. This vertical integration enables us to provide a comprehensive solution to our clients' interior decoration needs.
